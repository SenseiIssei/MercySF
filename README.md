**Mercy SF** is a free, cross‑platform **Shakes & Fidget bot** (also known as an **SF bot** or **S&F bot**) that automates your entire *Shakes and Fidget* account — quests, arena, dungeons, fortress, underworld, pets, guild, and much more. It runs on **Windows, macOS, and Linux**, with a console/CLI build for servers.

## Why Mercy SF?

- **Truly hands‑off** — set it up once and let it play *Shakes & Fidget* for you 24/7.
- **Every major feature automated** — not just quests, but the whole game.
- **Uses the official S&F API** — it is **not** a game mod or client injection; it talks to the same API your browser does.
- **Multi‑character** — run several accounts at the same time.
- **Cross‑platform** — Windows, macOS (Intel & Apple Silicon), and Linux, plus a headless CLI.
- **Auto‑updating** — the desktop app keeps itself up to date, and your settings are always preserved.

## FAQ

**What is Mercy SF?**
Mercy SF is an automation bot for *Shakes & Fidget* that plays the game for you — quests, arena, dungeons, fortress, pets, guild, and more. Learn more at **[mercysf.app](https://mercysf.app/)**.

**Is it a Shakes and Fidget mod?**
No. Mercy SF is a standalone **SF bot**, not a mod or client modification. It automates your account through the same official S&F API your browser already uses.

**Which platforms are supported?**
Windows, macOS (Intel & Apple Silicon), and Linux, plus a console/CLI build. Download at **[mercysf.app/#downloads](https://mercysf.app/#downloads)**.

**Is there a free version?**
Yes — there is a free tier. Premium and Supporter tiers add the advanced features. See **[mercysf.app](https://mercysf.app/#pricing)**.
